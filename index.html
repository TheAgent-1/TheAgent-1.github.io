Robotic Rocket Launcher

This remote-controlled robot, built with an Arduino microcontroller, is designed to launch model rockets, inspired by NASA's Crawler Transporter. It boasts a sturdy and robust chassis, capable of handling the weight and force of launching a model rocket. The robot is operated via a remote control, allowing for precise maneuvering and placement of the rocket before launch. This unique project combines the excitement of model rocketry with the technology of Arduino and robotics, creating a truly one-of-a-kind experience.
